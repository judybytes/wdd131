Site Plan: Discover South Africa

1. Site Name

Name: Discover South Africa

Reason: The name reflects the purpose of the website, which is to showcase South Africa's travel destinations, culture, and heritage.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide an informative guide to South Africa, targeting travelers, cultural enthusiasts, and those curious about the country. It will offer insights into cultural traditions, tourist destinations, and practical travel tips.

3. Scenarios

  • What are the best destinations to visit in South Africa for first-time travelers?
  • What cultural experiences can I explore while traveling in South Africa?

4. Color Schema

  • Deep Green (#006400): Used for headers and accents to symbolize nature.
  • pastel yellow (2cc8e): Used for highlights and call-to-action buttons to reflect warmth and richness.

5. Typography

  • Header Font: Roboto Slab, used for headings and titles.
  • Body Font: Open Sans, used for paragraphs and general content.

6. Wireframes

Mobile View: Vertical layout with collapsible sections for easy navigation.

Desktop View: Wider layout with a horizontal navigation bar and side-by-side sections for content.
